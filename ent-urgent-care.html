When to Choose Urgent Care

Is ENT Urgent Care Right for You?

Use this guide to determine whether ENT Urgent Care or a scheduled Capital ENT appointment is the best fit for your situation.

Ear Urgencies

  • Sudden ear pain or pressure
  • Ear infection with fever
  • Object stuck in the ear canal
  • Sudden hearing loss in one ear
  • Ear drainage or discharge
  • Earwax blockage causing discomfort

Nose & Sinus Urgencies

  • Severe sinus pressure or facial pain
  • Nosebleed that won't stop
  • Foreign object in the nose
  • Acute sinusitis
  • Nasal fracture after injury
  • Sudden loss of smell

Throat Urgencies

  • Severe sore throat or difficulty swallowing
  • Suspected strep throat
  • Throat abscess
  • Swollen tonsils with pain
  • Hoarseness lasting more than a few days

Emergency? If you are experiencing a life-threatening emergency, difficulty breathing, severe bleeding, or swallowing difficulty that prevents eating or drinking, please call 911 or go to your nearest emergency room immediately.

ENT Urgent Care vs. Scheduled Appointment

Availability Same day, walk-in & online booking Scheduled — same/next day often available
Best for Acute symptoms, sudden onset, conditions that can't wait Chronic conditions, surgical consultation, complex diagnosis, follow-up care
