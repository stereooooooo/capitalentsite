ENT Urgent Care Partnership

Can't Wait? Don't Go to General Urgent Care.

General urgent care clinics see everything from fractures to stomach bugs — ENT is a small slice of their training. For ear, nose, and throat problems, a specialist changes the outcome.

  • Proper equipment. ENT urgent care uses rhinoscopes, otomicroscopes, and laryngoscopes. General UC usually has only a basic otoscope.
  • Fewer unnecessary antibiotics. Specialists correctly distinguish viral from bacterial — reducing wasted courses and antibiotic resistance.
  • Same-day in-office procedures. Earwax removal under microscope, nosebleed cauterization, foreign body removal — general UC often refers these out.
  • Seamless escalation. If surgery or advanced imaging is needed, you're referred directly to Capital ENT — no starting over.
