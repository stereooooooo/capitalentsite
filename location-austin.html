Our Flagship Location

Capital ENT's Main Office in North Austin

Since 1993, Capital ENT & Sinus Center has called North Austin home. Our main office on the N Mopac frontage road near Braker Lane is the hub of the practice — where the largest team of ENT specialists in Central Texas sees patients five days a week for every ear, nose, and throat condition.

This is the only Capital ENT location with all four ancillary service lines on site: a low-dose CT scanner for immediate diagnostic imaging, a full audiology suite for hearing evaluations and hearing aid fittings, an allergy lab for skin-prick testing and immunotherapy programs, and coordination of at-home sleep studies for obstructive sleep apnea diagnosis.

We serve patients throughout the greater Austin area including Cedar Park, Round Rock, Pflugerville, Georgetown, and North Austin neighborhoods. Free parking is available directly in front of the building.

Beyond Standard ENT Care

On-Site Ancillary Services in Austin

Every Capital ENT location treats the full range of ear, nose, and throat conditions. Our Austin office also provides these specialized diagnostic and treatment services on site — so you spend less time traveling between appointments.

In-Office CT Imaging

Our low-dose Xoran MiniCAT CT scanner provides high-resolution sinus and temporal bone images in under a minute — right in the exam room. No separate imaging appointment, no waiting days for results. Your doctor reviews the scan with you on the spot to guide your treatment plan.

At-Home Sleep Studies

Suspect sleep apnea? We coordinate FDA-approved at-home sleep testing so you can complete your study in the comfort of your own bed. A board-certified sleep medicine physician interprets results, and your ENT doctor discusses treatment options — from CPAP to surgical alternatives like Inspire therapy.

Audiology & Hearing Aids

Our audiologist performs comprehensive hearing evaluations, tympanometry, and hearing aid fittings. We carry a full range of hearing aid brands and styles, from invisible-in-canal devices to rechargeable behind-the-ear models, with ongoing adjustments and support included.

Allergy Testing & Immunotherapy

Central Texas is one of the toughest allergy environments in the country. Our Austin office provides skin-prick allergy testing to identify your specific triggers, followed by customized immunotherapy — available as traditional allergy shots or convenient sublingual drops you take at home.
