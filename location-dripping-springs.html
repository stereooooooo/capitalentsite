On-Site Ancillary Service

Sleep Apnea Care in Dripping Springs

Living in the Hill Country means an active lifestyle — hiking, biking, swimming, and spending time outdoors. Untreated sleep apnea can rob you of the energy and focus you need to enjoy it. Dr. Myhill can evaluate your symptoms, coordinate a sleep study you complete at home, and build a treatment plan that fits your life.

At-Home Sleep Studies

Skip the sleep lab. Our FDA-approved at-home sleep test lets you complete the study in your own bed with a portable monitor. A board-certified sleep medicine physician reviews the data, and Dr. Myhill goes over the results at your next visit in Dripping Springs.

CPAP Therapy

For moderate to severe sleep apnea, CPAP therapy is the first-line treatment. Dr. Myhill helps you find the right mask style and pressure settings so you can sleep comfortably and wake up refreshed.

CPAP Alternatives

If CPAP isn't working for you, alternatives exist — from oral appliances and positional therapy to surgical options tailored to your anatomy. Dr. Myhill evaluates each patient individually to find the most effective approach.

Inspire Therapy Evaluation

Inspire is an FDA-approved implantable device for moderate to severe sleep apnea in patients who can't use CPAP. Dr. Myhill, a fellowship-trained surgeon, can assess your candidacy and coordinate the evaluation process.

All ENT conditions are treated at this location. CT imaging, audiology, and allergy testing are available at our Austin and Lakeway offices.
