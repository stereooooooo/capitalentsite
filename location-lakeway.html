Lake Travis Community

Specialized ENT Care on the Shores of Lake Travis

The Lakeway–Bee Cave corridor is one of the fastest-growing communities in Central Texas, and Capital ENT has been part of it for years. Our Lakeway office at Lakeway Centre gives residents access to board-certified ENT specialists without the commute into Austin — two physicians, two PAs, and a dedicated audiologist see patients five days a week.

Whether you're dealing with chronic sinusitis, hearing loss, childhood ear infections, snoring, or any other ear, nose, or throat condition, you'll receive the same level of care delivered at our flagship Austin location. The office also features an in-office CT scanner and a full audiology suite so diagnostic workups can be completed in a single visit.

We serve patients throughout the Lake Travis area including Lakeway, Bee Cave, Spicewood, West Lake Hills, The Hills, and Lago Vista. Free parking is available at Lakeway Centre.

Beyond Standard ENT Care

On-Site Ancillary Services in Lakeway

Every Capital ENT location treats the full range of ear, nose, and throat conditions. Our Lakeway office also provides these specialized services on site. Allergy testing and immunotherapy are available at our Austin office.

In-Office CT Imaging

Our Lakeway office has the same low-dose CT scanner as our Austin location — providing high-resolution sinus and temporal bone images in under a minute. Your doctor reviews results with you immediately, eliminating the need for a separate imaging center visit.

Audiology & Hearing Aids

Audiologist Jill Sullivan, AuD, provides comprehensive hearing evaluations, tympanometry, and hearing aid fittings at our Lakeway office. We carry all major hearing aid brands with ongoing adjustments and follow-up care included.

At-Home Sleep Studies

If you or your doctor suspect sleep apnea, we can coordinate an FDA-approved at-home sleep test. Complete the study in your own bed, then return to our Lakeway office to discuss results and treatment options with your ENT specialist.
