On-Site Ancillary Service

Sleep Apnea Diagnosis & Treatment in Marble Falls

Obstructive sleep apnea affects millions of Americans and is linked to high blood pressure, heart disease, daytime fatigue, and reduced quality of life. Dr. Myhill can evaluate your symptoms, order an at-home sleep study, and develop a personalized treatment plan — all from our Marble Falls office.

At-Home Sleep Studies

Complete your sleep study in the comfort of your own bed with a portable monitoring device. No overnight stay at a sleep lab required. A board-certified sleep medicine physician interprets your results, and Dr. Myhill reviews them with you at your follow-up appointment.

CPAP Therapy

CPAP remains the gold standard for moderate to severe obstructive sleep apnea. Dr. Myhill works with you to find the right mask and pressure settings to maximize comfort and compliance.

CPAP Alternatives

Can't tolerate CPAP? Multiple alternatives exist, from oral appliance therapy and positional devices to minimally invasive surgical options. Dr. Myhill can evaluate which approach is right for your anatomy and severity level.

Inspire Therapy Evaluation

Inspire is an FDA-approved implantable device for patients with moderate to severe sleep apnea who cannot tolerate CPAP. Dr. Myhill can determine if you're a candidate and coordinate the evaluation process.

All ENT conditions are treated at this location. CT imaging, audiology, and allergy testing are available at our Austin and Lakeway offices.
