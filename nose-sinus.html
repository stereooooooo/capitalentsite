Our Most Requested Nasal Procedures

Treatments That Get You Breathing Again

01

Balloon Sinuplasty

A minimally invasive in-office procedure to open blocked sinus passages — no cutting, no packing, rapid recovery.

Balloon sinuplasty uses a small, flexible balloon catheter to open blocked sinus passageways and facilitate drainage. Unlike traditional surgery, it doesn't remove bone or tissue. Most patients return to normal activity within 24–48 hours.

Learn More
02

Endoscopic Sinus Surgery

Advanced surgery to treat chronic sinusitis without the pain and complications of older techniques.

Functional endoscopic sinus surgery (FESS) can now be performed without the pain and complications of previous sinus surgeries. Using a tiny camera and specialized instruments, our surgeons remove blockages while preserving healthy tissue.

Learn More
03

Septoplasty

Correcting a deviated septum using cutting-edge techniques — no harsh packing, faster recovery.

We utilize cutting-edge surgical techniques to correct the nasal septum without the need for harsh packing and splinting. This reduces pain, discomfort, and recovery time significantly. Most patients notice improved breathing almost immediately.

Learn More
04

Turbinate Reduction

A minimally invasive treatment to reduce nasal obstruction, chronic congestion, and sinus pressure.

Often used together with other procedures, turbinate reduction eliminates chronic sinus pain and pressure, headaches, nasal congestion, and sleep disorders. The minimally invasive procedure reduces the size of the turbinate bones to open nasal passages.

More Nasal Specialties

Additional Nose & Sinus Services We Offer

Nasal Polypectomy

We treat nasal polyps and address underlying causes such as allergies, using a minimally invasive surgical technique that requires no painful postoperative nasal packing.

Nasal Obstruction

Comprehensive evaluation and treatment of nasal obstruction from any cause — structural, inflammatory, or related to allergies — so you can breathe freely again.

Chronic Sinusitis Management

Medical and surgical management of chronic sinusitis, including prescription therapy, allergy testing, and surgical intervention when medications aren't enough.

Pediatric Nasal Care

Specialized nasal and sinus care for children, including treatment for recurrent sinus infections, nasal obstruction, adenoid problems, and other pediatric nasal conditions.

Sinus Pressure & Headaches

Evaluation and treatment of chronic sinus pressure and sinus headaches, identifying whether the cause is structural, inflammatory, or allergy-related.

Cedar Fever & Allergic Rhinitis

Austin is one of the highest allergen count cities in the US. We offer in-house allergy testing and customized immunotherapy plans to help you get lasting relief.
