Latif M. Dharamsi, MD
Board-Certified Otolaryngologist
Sinusitis Nasal Obstruction Pediatric ENT

Dr. Dharamsi is a board-certified ENT specialist who treats a wide range of conditions, including nasal obstruction, chronic sinus infections, sleep apnea, and chronic ear conditions. He cares for patients of all ages, from infants to adults, and focuses on identifying the root cause of each issue. Dr. Dharamsi emphasizes minimally invasive treatments like balloon sinuplasty and submucosal turbinate reduction. Passionate about helping patients breathe and feel better, he has been consistently recognized as one of Austin's Best ENT providers.

Jeffrey Allen Myhill, MD, FACS, FARS
Otolaryngologist & Facial Plastic Surgeon
Sinus Surgery Sleep Surgery Facial Plastic Surgery

Dr. Myhill specializes in the treatment of sinus and nasal issues utilizing the latest innovative technology, including minimally invasive in-office procedures. He also performs more traditional sinus surgery for patients experiencing chronic sinus problems not amenable to an in-office procedure. Dr. Myhill provides the full range of sleep surgery for the treatment of obstructive sleep apnea.
