Most Common Concern

Sleep Apnea — A Serious Condition That Can Be Treated

Sleep apnea is a serious medical condition that can worsen over time and become life-threatening when not treated. It occurs when your breathing ceases numerous times while you are asleep — sometimes 30 times per hour or more.

We offer comprehensive diagnosis and a full range of surgical and non-surgical sleep apnea treatments tailored to your specific situation.

What We Treat

The Most Common Throat & Sleep Conditions

Sleep Apnea

Sleep apnea causes your breathing to repeatedly stop during sleep — sometimes hundreds of times per night. Left untreated, it significantly increases risk of heart disease, stroke, and other serious conditions. We offer comprehensive diagnosis and both surgical and non-surgical treatment options.

Learn More

Snoring

Injection snoreplasty is a minimally invasive treatment for snoring with little to no pain involved. We also offer surgical snoring treatments. Loud, chronic snoring can be a sign of sleep apnea — we'll evaluate the underlying cause and recommend the most effective treatment.

Learn More

Tonsillitis & Tonsillectomy

Recurring or chronic tonsillitis, or enlarged tonsils that make it hard to breathe, can now be treated with a tonsillectomy performed as an outpatient procedure using modern techniques to reduce pain and speed recovery. We care for both adults and children.

Learn More

Voice & Throat Disorders

Our voice is the instrument we use to communicate. If that instrument isn't working, it affects everything we do. We provide thorough evaluation of the entire throat with a painless fiber optic telescope and treat both medical and surgical throat disorders.

Learn More
Our Throat & Sleep Services

Comprehensive Throat & Sleep Care Services

Sleep Study & Diagnosis

We work with accredited sleep labs to provide comprehensive sleep studies that accurately diagnose sleep apnea and other sleep-disordered breathing conditions.

Sleep Apnea Surgery

Multiple surgical options including uvulopalatopharyngoplasty (UPPP), soft palate procedures, and nasal surgery to treat obstructive sleep apnea.

Injection Snoreplasty

A minimally invasive in-office procedure to treat snoring by injecting a solution into the soft palate, causing slight scarring that stiffens tissue to reduce vibration.

Throat Care & Evaluation

Comprehensive throat evaluations using a painless fiber optic telescope for diagnosing and treating chronic sore throats, laryngitis, and throat lesions.

Voice Care

Specialized care for voice disorders including vocal cord nodules, polyps, and paralysis. We treat singers, teachers, and other professional voice users.

Tonsil & Adenoid Surgery

Modern outpatient tonsillectomy and adenoidectomy techniques that minimize pain and recovery time for both children and adults with recurrent tonsillitis.
